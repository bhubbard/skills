# Jetpack VideoPress Library

## Overview
The VideoPress Dashboard is a centralized space within WordPress to upload, organize, and manage your video library efficiently.

## Features
- **Centralized Management**: View and edit video details, cover images, and privacy settings in one place.
- **Filtering**: Filter your library by rating or privacy setting.
- **Unlimited Logins**: Work collaboratively with a team without per-seat charges.
- **Local Uploads**: Upload local videos to the Jetpack cloud library.

## Instructions
1. Access the VideoPress Dashboard from the WordPress admin menu.
2. View your entire video collection in multiple display formats.
3. Select a video to update its metadata (title, description, cover image, and privacy).
4. Organize your library to keep your website's media clean and easy to navigate.
