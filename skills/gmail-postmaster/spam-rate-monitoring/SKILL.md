# Spam Rate Monitoring

Use this skill when needing to monitor or troubleshoot high spam rates reported by Gmail users.

## Instructions
1. Check the Spam Rate dashboard in Postmaster Tools.
2. Identify days with spikes in user spam complaints.
3. Correlate the dates with specific email campaigns.
4. Suggest content or targeting improvements to lower the complaint rate below Google's recommended threshold (0.10%).
