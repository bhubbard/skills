# Filevine Billing Contacts Skill

This skill outlines the process for managing billing contacts, tracking expenses, and ensuring accurate invoicing.

## Core Capabilities

- **Contact Association**: Linking individuals and organizations as billing contacts on a project.
- **Invoice Tracking**: Monitoring the status of generated invoices.
- **Expense Logging**: Accurately recording project-related expenses.
- **Payment Processing**: Documenting received payments and applying them to open invoices.

## Best Practices
1. Verify the billing contact's information before finalizing any invoice.
2. Always attach relevant receipts when logging expenses.
3. Communicate clearly with the client regarding payment expectations and deadlines.
