# Filevine Support Channels

This skill outlines the standard procedures for interacting with Filevine Support.

## Core Channels

1.  **Filevine Support Tickets:**
    *   **Use Case:** Bug reports, complex technical issues, feature requests, and account management queries.
    *   **Action:** Submit tickets via the Filevine Help Center or directly via email to support.
    *   **Information Required:** Org ID, Project ID, specific error messages, steps to reproduce, and screenshots/videos if possible.

2.  **Filevine Community:**
    *   **Use Case:** Peer-to-peer discussions, best practice inquiries, and general how-to questions.
    *   **Action:** Access the Filevine Community portal.

3.  **Customer Success Manager (CSM):**
    *   **Use Case:** Strategic discussions, contract renewals, escalations, and broad training needs.
    *   **Action:** Reach out directly to the assigned CSM.

## Best Practices
*   Always gather the Org ID and Project ID before submitting a request.
*   Clearly articulate the business impact of the issue.
